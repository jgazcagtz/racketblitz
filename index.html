How to Play

Welcome to Racket Blitz 🏓

Objective: Be the first to reach 5 points by hitting the ball past your opponent's paddle.

Controls:
Player 1 (Left Paddle): W (Up), S (Down)
Player 2 (Right Paddle): Up Arrow (Up), Down Arrow (Down)

On mobile devices, use the on-screen buttons.

Game Modes:
- 1 Player: Play against the computer with selectable difficulty levels.
- 2 Players: Play against a friend.

Power-Ups:
- 🛡️ Shield: Increase your paddle size temporarily.
- ⚡ Lightning: Increase your paddle speed temporarily.
- ❌ Shrink: Decrease opponent's paddle size temporarily.

Good luck and have fun!
